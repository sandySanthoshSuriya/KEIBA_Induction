Login/Logout from Others Tab

User can login/logout from the others tab based on the login/logout status.

Login

Step 1 : App should be in logout status

Step 2 : By clicking on “Others TAB”, screen will navigates to others page

Step 3 : If app is in logout status, “Login(ローソン)” option will display in others tab

Step 4 : When Click on “Login” option, it navigates to Login screen

Step 5 : Enter vaild username and click on “Next”

Step 6 : Enter valid password and click on “Next”

Step 7 : Once logged in, user redirects to “Other” tab. “Login” option changed to “Logout” (ログアウト)

To Logout:

Step 1 : App should be in login status

Step 2 : By clicking on “Others TAB”,screen navigate to others page

Step 3 : If app is in login status, can see the “Logout”(ログアウト) in others tab

Step 4 : When Click on “Logout” option, confirmation popup will open

Step 5 : By click on
Cancel - retain in same page (others tab).
Logout - user will be logged out and and the status changed to “Login”

Deposit

Deposit is used to deposit the amount from the Rakuten bank account to Rakuten Wallet. The charge amount will be added to Rakuten Wallet.

Deposit/Charge can be done from same Tab.

Deposit - Deposit money to Rakuten Keiba via Rakuten Bank Account
Charge - Charge account to Rakuten Keiba via Rakuten points or PayPay.

Deposit can be completed via the screens below

  • Deposit Instructions
  • Deposit Instruction Confirmation
  • Deposit Completion

Note : Keiba members will only be able to make Deposit Associate and Rakuten members unable to make Deposit, will redirect to the registration screen when Deposit tab is clicked.

Steps to followed

Step 1 : Launch the Keiba APP

Step 2 : Login as a Keiba member and Click Others TAB and click Deposit/Charge button

Step 3 : The screen navigates to Deposit instruction screen

Step 4 : Click drop down and select Rakuten bank

Step 5 : After selecting the Rakuten bank the user will get the below page

Step 6 : Based on the selection, Input amount in the 指示額 field.

If Rakuten bank selected – enter the amount in 指示額 field (in 100 denominations)

Turn on the メール通知 toggle (notification mail will be received for linked mail ID)
Turn off the メール通知 toggle (notification mail will not be received for linked mail ID)

Step 7 : After Entering the amount click confirm button

Step 8 : Screen navigates to Deposit Instruction Confirmation Screen

Step 9 : Enter the PIN(1234) and Click Deposit button

Step 10 : Screen will navigate to the Deposit Completion screen with successful Deposit completion.
Then the Deposit amount added to Keiba wallet

APP Settlement

This is used to transfer the amount from Rakuten Wallet to Rakuten Bank account.

There are 3 screens being used for Settlement in the Rakuten Keiba application.

  • Settlement Instructions
  • Settlement Instruction Confirmation
  • Settlement Instruction Result

Note : keiba member will only able to make Settlement, for Associate and Rakuten member unable to make settlement, will get the registration screen while click the Settlement button

Step to followed

Step 1 : Connect VPN in any STG and launch the keiba APP

Step 2 : Login as keiba member and click Others TAB and click Settlement

Step 3 : After click Settlement button the user will get the Settlement Instructions screen

Step 4 : here turn on the mail notification toggle(notification mail will be received for linked mail ID) and click confirm button If turn off the mail notification toggle(notification mail will not be received for linked mail ID)

Step 5 : The user will get the Settlement Instruction Confirmation Screen

Step 6 : Here enter the PIN [1234] and Click Settlement button

Step 7 : The user will get Settlement completion Screen, the Amount will be settled to rakuten bank and balance section should be updated

APP Charge

Charge function allows you to charge your account to Rakuten Keiba using Rakuten Points or PayPay. The charge amount will be added to Rakuten Wallet.

Deposit/Charge can be done from same Tab.
Deposit - Deposit money to Rakuten Keiba via Rakuten Bank Account
Charge - Charge account to Rakuten Keiba via Rakuten points or PayPay.

Charge can be completed via the screens below

  • Charge Instructions
  • Charge Instruction Confirmation
  • Charge Completion

Note : Keiba members will only be able to make Charge Associate and Rakuten members unable to make charge, will redirect to the registration screen when Charge tab is clicked.

Step to followed

Step 1 : Launch the Keiba APP

Step 2 : Login as a Keiba member and Click Others TAB and click Deposit/Charge button

Step 3 : The screen navigates to Charge instruction screen

Step 4 : Click drop down and select Rakuten point/ PayPay Note : The user should have enough Rakuten points / PayPay balance

Step 5 : After selecting the Rakuten points/PayPay the user will get the below page

Step 6 : Based on the selection, Input amount in the 指示額 field.
If Rakuten points selected - check the available points earned and enter the amount in 指示額 field (in 100 denominations)
If PayPay selected – enter the amount in 指示額 field (in 100 denominations)
Turn on the メール通知 toggle (notification mail will be received for linked mail ID)
Turn off the メール通知 toggle (notification mail will not be received for linked mail ID)

Step 7 : After Entering the amount click confirm button

Step 8 : Screen navigates to Charge Instruction Confirmation Screen

Step 9 : Enter the PIN(1234) and Click Charge button

Step 10 : Screen will navigate to the Charge Completion screen with successful charge completion. Then the charge amount added to Keiba wallet.

Notification Settings

Users can change the Keiba app's notification settings and favorite settings through the settings icon in the Others tab.

Click Others -> Settings -> Notification settings

Users can enable or disable notifications based on user types using the provided toggle button.

The below 3 members can only able to receive notification from Rakuten Keiba system.

  • Not login/Guest user
  • Rakuten
  • Associate Members
Not login/Guest user

User can able to login by clicking Login button.

Rakuten member and Associate member
Keiba Members

Only Keiba members can access all the settings, including favorite horse and jockey notifications, lottery confirmation, and grade race notifications. Using the provided toggles, a Keiba member can enable or disable the following notifications.

Favorite Settings

If a Keiba member selects a jockey or horse as their favorite, they will receive notifications when their favorite jockey or horse is scheduled to ride the next day.

The Keiba member can enable or disable favorite notifications using the toggles.

Note : Associate member can able to set the favorite horse and jockey but cannot use the notification settings for favorite.

Similarly, if a Keiba member receives a refund for a purchased betting ticket or for a grade race, the corresponding toggles can be used to enable or disable notifications from the Keiba app.

To Listen
Normal Bet

Step 1 : Select Bet Tab and choose any race course.

Step 2 : After selected race course choose 通常投票(Normal) betting type

Step 3 : Select Betting type

Step 4 : Select any betting formula.

Step 5 : Choose any horse and click セット(Set) or展開セット( Set to deploy ) button Click 金額入力へ (go to Amount input) button

Step 6 : Enter the amount in Text box and click [ 投票内容を確認する] (Check your vote) button

Step 7 : After click [ 投票内容を確認する] navigate to confirmation page and Re-Enter the amount and click 投票する


Step 8 : After click 投票する Button it will navigate to bet completion page. Here Wallet amount and cart count to be updated

To Listen
Odds Bet

Step 1 : Select Bet Tab and choose any race course

Step 2 : After selected race course choose betting type as ODDS

Step 3 : After selecting betting types odds, screen navigate to formula selection page

Step 4 : Select any formula and any horse Number.

Step 5 : After selected betting formula and horse number click [ 金額入力へ] button

Step 6 : Enter the amount in Text box and click [ 投票内容を確認する] button

Step 7 : After click [ 投票内容を確認する] screen navigates to confirmation page and Re-Enter the amount to confirm and click 投票する

Step 8 : After click 投票する, screen navigates to bet completion page

To Listen
Lite Bet

Step 1 : Select the Racecourse from BET Tab.

Step 2 : Select Betting Type as Bet Lite from which race we want to Bet. Example(1R to 12 R)

Step 3 : Select Betting Formula and Betting Method and click 買い目を選択する > (The Race track and Race Number are automatically displayed based on the previous selection.)

  1. 競馬場 (Racetrack)
  2. レース (Race Number)
  3. 式別 (Betting Formula)
  4. 方式 (Betting Method)

Step 4 : 1) Select the horses we want to Bet 2) Enter the amount we want to bet and click 投票内容を確認する

Step 5 : Re-enter the Betting amount to confirm and click 投票する(vote) button.

Step 6 : Betting has completed and screen moves to Bet completion page.

To Listen

Ensure that mobile date time and region should be in Japan zone

Steps to display review popup screen:
  • Open App setting
  • Go to General
  • Click on Date & Time
  • Turn off "Set automatically."
  • Increment date by one manually
  • Open the Keiba app
  • Put the Keiba app in background
  • Repeat Step 5 to 7 until the Popup display

To Listen
Popup Screen Transition

Screen A (Review Popup)Are you enjoying the Rakuten Horsing App

Option A-->NO Moves to Screen D (Request feedback)
Option B-->YES Moves to Screen B(Request a Review)

Screen B(Request a Review) -Request for review can you write a review on the Play Store

In this popup screen there will be three options
Option ATo Review screen (Moves to Play Store/App Store Review)
Option B I will do it later (Popup closed and Moves to Home screen)
** You can again follow the steps to display the popup, The popup will display.
Option C--> No thanks (Popup closed and Moves to Home screen) ** Once the “No Thanks” clicked the popup will not be displayed again, until the APP is reinstalled

Screen C (Play Store/App Store Review Screen)

Note : This option will only work on Japan ids only. When we access from India an error message will be displayed in Play Store and Appstore stated as “unable to write a review”

Screen D (Request Feedback): Do you have any feedback or requests

Option A-->No thanks (Popup closed and Moves to Home screen)
Option B-->Yes (Moves to Comments Screen

Screen E (Comments Screen): Feedback requests

1. X--> Popup closed and Moves to Home screen.
2. Enter Comments & Submit button the following popup (Comments Completion screen) will appear

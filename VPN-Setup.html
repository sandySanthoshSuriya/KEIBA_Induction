Steps to be Followed to Connect Sophos


Step 1 : Open the URL https://115.244.222.150:8443/ in your browser. The below portal will open

Step 2 : Type Your Username and Password and Click Login.

Step 3 : Select OTP tokens from the left-side menu.

Step 4 : Download google authenticator on your mobile and scan the QR code for OTP.

Step 5 : Open the URL https://115.244.222.150:8443/userportal/webpages/myaccount/index.jsp in your browser. The below page will open.

Step 6 : Select VPN from the left-side menu.

Step 7 : Sophos Connect Client-Windows Users download for Windows/Mac users download for macOS, application.

Step 8 : Accept the Sophos End User agreement and Click Install. (Some Systems will ask Yes/No for Install. Select Yes).

Step 9 : Once Installation is Completed click Finish.

Step 10 : Select windows icon and open the Sophos Connect application

Step 11 : Click Arrow and click the shown Sophos Connect icon.

Step 12 : Click Import connection.

Step 13 : Browse and Select the xxxxxxxxxx__ssl_vpn_config (which we downloaded with your name earlier) and select open.

Step 14 : Below Shown screen will open select Connect.

Step 15 : Enter your Username in the username column.

Step 16 : Enter your Password + Google Authenticator OTP Continuously without space in the password column and click sign in.

Step 17 : Once Connected Will get this screen and start your work.

Step 18 : Select Personal-Change password from the left-side menu and change the password immediately.
